Rachel Harris is a Boston-based illustrator and designer. She holds a MFA in graphic design from RISD, and a BFA in visual communications from Washington University. She previously worked for the Yale School of Drama and St. Louis Magazine. Rachel currently works at Fathom.

Rachel's illustration clients include The New York Times, Time Out New York, The Village Voice, Chicago Magazine, Baltimore Magazine, The Washington Post, The Boston Globe, and the Girl Scouts. You can follow more of her drawings on instagram @laraquelx.
